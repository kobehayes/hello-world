About

Hello!

My name is Kobe.

I'm a multimedia artist and graphic designer from Mississippi. I have a B.A. in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis. I like to explore the relationship between music and visual arts.

My life revolves around my hobbies. Everything I do is a means to fund my hobbies.

I've played percussion since middle school and am now formally trained. More recently, I've picked up guitar and synthesis.

I've always enjoyed drawing, but I didn't decide to study art until third semester of college. I studied graphic design, but I also took many studio art courses. I found that I was drawn to the more open-ended approach of making art rather than the stricter confines of graphic design. My work explores the boundary between design and art as a means of emotional connection. I emphasize craft and detail in my work. I take inspiration from visual artists such as Ai Weiwei, Kehide Wiley, Marina Abromovic, Marcel Duchamp, Max Ernst, and many others from the surrealist movement. Some of the musicians that influence my work are Bjork, Imogen Heap, SOPHIE, and the Twilight soundtracks.
