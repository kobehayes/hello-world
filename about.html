Hello!

My name is Kobe.

I'm a multimedia artist, graphic designer, and musician from Gulfport, Mississippi. I have a B.A. in Communication Design from Washington University in St. Louis. I enjoy exploring a wide range of visual mediums including sound, painting, --

My primary instrument is percussion, but I also play the guitar and keyboard. I minored in music, so I am familiar with music theory and composition. I am also familiar with the production side of music. I have experience with recording and mixing inside a DAW--

My approach to design is influenced by-- I emphasize craftmanship and detail in my work. I take inspiration from visual artists such as Ai Weiwei, Kehide Wiley, Marina Abramovic, Marcel Duchamp, Max Ernst, and many others from the surrealist movement.
