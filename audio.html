___Recordings

Silo

2022

An abstract sound piece that uses studio recordings and outdoor audio samples from a field microphone. This piece simulates the sonic and spatial qualities of the inside of an empty silo through intricate stereo mixing with programmed automation. Mixed and mastered in Logic Pro.

Molasses

2022

An abstract composition made with studio recordings of bowed vibraphone, classical guitar, xylophone, and piano. Mixed and mastered in Logic Pro.

Scrape

2022

Rhythmic counterpart to Molasses, recorded in the same session. Mixed and mastered in Logic Pro.
